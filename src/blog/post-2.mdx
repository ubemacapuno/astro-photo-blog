Superior, AZ is about 45 minutes east of Phoenix, and it's a popular spot for climbing with sport climbing in "Atlantis" and "The Pond", and bouldering around Oak Flat Campground. <br /><br />

Oak Flat has also become the center of a major legislative and [environmental controversy tied to proposed copper mining](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Resolution_Copper). Federal land-transfer provisions included in defense legislation would allow Resolution Copper to develop an underground mine beneath Oak Flat, raising concerns about land subsidence, water use, and the permanent loss of climbing areas and culturally significant sites. <br /><br />
