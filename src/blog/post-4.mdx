The [Titan Missile Museum](https://titanmissilemuseum.org/) is a former ICBM site located in Sahuarita, Arizona, just south of Tucson. The site became operational in 1963 and was deactivated in 1984. An inert Titan II missile remains inside the silo. Walking through the museum felt like stepping into an â€™80s Cold War time capsule.

<br />

CineStill 800T was perfect for capturing the fluorescent lighting, and its halation gives the images a distinct, almost Star Wars lightsaber-like feel. I definitely recommend this film stock for this location; I brought two rolls and shot with my Pentax KX, and my fiance used my Ricoh FF90.

<br />

You can view a map on the Titan Missile Museum website [here](https://titanmissilemuseum.org/museum/map/).
